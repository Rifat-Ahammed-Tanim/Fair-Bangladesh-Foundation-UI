Who We Are

Fair Bangladesh Foundation is a social initiative that works to tackle the clean water crisis and empower socially vulnerable communities in Bangladesh, especially in coastal areas. Our journey began in 2021, amidst the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, which highlighted the vulnerabilities faced by these communities. Witnessing this marginalization, we felt compelled to take action.

With a specific focus on research-based development, we design and implement innovative and non-traditional projects that create impact on millions. We believe that by understanding the challenges faced by marginalized groups, we can develop more effective interventions. As a social initiative, our approach is threefold: Assessment, Action, and Empowerment. Along with it, we're committed to advocating for change at the policy level. Through engaging with various stakeholders and participating in policy dialogues, we try to amplify the voices of marginalized communities and influence decision-making processes in Bangladesh. Here, we're more than just an organization; we're a community of passionate individuals dedicated to creating a brighter, more equitable future for all. And we believe that only effective social actions can ensure this ‘equitable future’ for all of us.

Mission

Our mission is to create lasting solutions that promote resilience and inclusivity, creating a future where every person has the opportunity to thrive.

Vision

Fair Bangladesh Foundation envisions a world where every community in coastal Bangladesh is empowered to sustain its own growth and overcome social challenges. We look for a future where basic rights including education, health, food, and clean water are accessible to all, and where marginalized groups are empowered to thrive.

Founding Story

During the Covid-19 days, when the world went in isolation, we started to think of doing something outside the box. Immediately we could not come up with the full idea of creating a social initiative like Fair Bangladesh Foundation, but we were able to create a small framework for an organization like ours. In the meantime, our chief patron, Mr. Khalilur Rahman, collected some funds from his friends and families living in the UK and wanted to create a sustainable impact in coastal Bangladesh with his funds. This actually led us to open up our previous idea and implement multiple projects in climate-vulnerable coastal districts including Satkhira, Khulna, Pirojpur, and so on. Our glorious journey started in 2021, and since then, we are growing gradually and creating an impact on thousands of lives.
